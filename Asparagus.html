Asparagus


Photo by Markus Spiske on Unsplash




Author: Person.Margaret W Howard




Ingredients



asparagus 1 bunch
boiling water 3 quarts
butter 3 tablespoons
salt 1 tablespoon
pepper 1/3 teaspoon



instructions

  1. Vegetables should be thoroughly washed and cooked till tender in freshly boiling,
    salted water (1/2 tablespoon salt to 1 quart water)


    Photo by Stephanie Studer on Unsplash




  2. Remove white part of stalk and tie in bunches


    Photo by Sheila Joy on Unsplash




  3. Cook about 20 minutes


    Photo by Toa Heftiba on Unsplash




  4. When asparagus is soft, drain well and place on slices of buttered toast which have
    been previously dipped in the water in which the asparagus was cooked


    Photo by Louis Hansel on Unsplash




  5. Season with required seasonings


    Photo by Mgg Vitchakorn on Unsplash
